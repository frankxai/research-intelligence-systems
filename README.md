# Research Intelligence Systems

**Research Intelligence Systems** is the portfolio layer for agentic scientific workflows across literature review, evidence synthesis, claim graphs, research design, reproducibility, and open science.

## Portfolio

- Packs for domain areas (neuroscience, psychology, learning-science, cognitive-science, behavioral-science, consciousness-studies)
- Reusable workflows
- Schemas for papers, claims, evidence tables
- Built on Research Intelligence OS

## Key Concepts

- ResearchPack: packaging for research systems
- Claim graphs
- Evidence tables
- Reproducibility reviews
